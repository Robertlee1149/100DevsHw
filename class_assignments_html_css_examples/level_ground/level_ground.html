WHERE GRIT MEETS OPPORTUNITY

At Level Ground (LG), urban youth cultivate grit and resilience through Mixed Martial Arts. They learn to apply these values 'off the mat' in the pursuit of their ambitions through our Student Traner Certification and College Access programs.
